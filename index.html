IGNIS

Fire and Pixel Light performance

IGNIS is a fire performance team founded by students from Prince of Songkhla University. While the original club focused on combat performances, when it closed, three passionate fire twirlers reunited and formed a new team. We have gained experience performing full-scale fire shows at hotels and events both inside and outside Phuket, creating captivating stories told through flames.

We have also developed a new type of show: LED or Pixel Light. These shows are suitable for both indoor and outdoor performances, and can be creatively created with a variety of elements, including logos and images, to create memorable and engaging performances for the audience. IGNIS has earned the trust of our customers because we value feedback and are always ready to improve and develop our shows to the best of our ability.

Pixel Light
Performance

We also incorporate lighting synchronization techniques to the beat of the music, adding depth and emotion to the show, making it both beautiful and engaging.
